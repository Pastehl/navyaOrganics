Order Process

1. Once your order has been placed you will be redirected to the User Page where you can track your order's status

2. The order status would be set to "Pending". Please allow us up to 24 hours to process your order.

3. Your order may be cancelled by the system due to the following reasons:

- Your user information is incomplete.

- Your delivery address is not serviced by our shipping partners

- The product you are trying to purchase is currently unavailable

If your order has been cancelled, its status would be marked as "Cancelled" along with the reason for cancellation

To order again, place your desired items in the Cart and click Checkout.

4. For COD payments, your order would be processed and its status can be seen on the user page.

5. For GCash payments, your order, if accepted, would have a status of "Awaiting Payment". Click the status and pay the exact amount then your order would be processed. Its status can be tracked in the user page.
